About

Dr. Patricia Celan is a resident physician in postgraduate psychiatry training.
She is also an abuse survivor.
And you can be, too.

She witnessed firsthand the atrocious impact that abuse can have on adults and children alike, and struggled to maneuver escape and recovery from the trauma.

Patricia started this website to raise awareness about the intricacies of abuse and how to get away from it. While escaping abuse may be difficult and most victims need support to get through it, survivors agree – a life free of abuse is worth the effort.
